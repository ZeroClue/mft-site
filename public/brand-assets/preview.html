Colors

2. Transfer Notation

Precision · Intention · Editorial

Colors

Typography

Lyon Display / Crimson Pro — Editorial serif. The bracket-plus is a proofreader's mark.

Usage

Precise, intentional, publishing aesthetic. Deep greens with gold accents.

3. The Weave

Connection · Craft · Elegance

Colors

Typography

Tiempos / Source Serif 4 — Contemporary serif. The weave creates the plus at crossing.

Usage

Elegant, connected, distinctive. Charcoal with brown undertones. The most iconic mark.
